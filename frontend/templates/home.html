About

Bankroll Me is a mult-billion dollar company that just launched 2 weeks ago. The premise is simple. You list the things you want, shamelessly, and wait to see if any friend or generous benefactors will donate to your cause. You can pay it forward by donating to another person's cause, anonymously or not.

How To

Sign up for an account, log in, and start creating a list of things you want or need. It's that simple.

You can also search for your friends and donate to their cause. Add them to your list of friends and their recent activity will populate your newsfeed.
